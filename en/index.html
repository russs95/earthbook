As we become more and more aware of the ecological crises caused by our modern age, we long to green our ways. However, what does 'green' mean? More importantly, what should it mean? Guided by the example of magnificent creatures and great cultures, this book lays out an ethics of green and grey based on Earth's planetary pattern.
