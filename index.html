Description

As we become more and more aware of the ecological challenges created by our modern world, we long to amend our ways, to do better, to be green. But how can we sure that 'green' is green? This book lays out an ecological ethic based on the Earth's example so that we can confidently reorient our homes and enterprises to enrich the ecosystems that they are a part.
